## What is BBP & Infection Control Training Tracker?

The BBP & Infection Control Training Tracker is a free browser-based tool that helps tattoo and piercing studios monitor staff bloodborne pathogen (BBP) and infection control certification dates. The tool stores all data locally in your browser using localStorage, meaning no information is uploaded to any server or shared with third parties.

Users can add staff records containing the staff member's name, certification type, completion date, renewal period in months, and optional notes. The tool automatically calculates the expiry date based on the completion date and renewal period. Each record displays a color-coded status badge indicating whether the certification is current, expiring within 60 days, or expired.

Records are sorted by expiry date with soonest expiring certifications appearing first. Users can export all records as a CSV file for offline storage or audit purposes, delete individual records, or clear the entire database.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Add a Staff Record

1. Enter the staff member's name in the **Staff Name** field
2. Select the certification type from the **Certification** dropdown menu
3. Choose the date the training was completed using the **Date Completed** date picker
4. The **Renewal (months)** field auto-fills based on certification type (12 months for annual, 24 months for biennial)
5. Optionally add notes such as certificate numbers or training provider URLs
6. Click **Add Record** to save

### Step 2: View Records

- All records appear in a table sorted by expiry date
- Each row shows: Name, Certification, Completed date, Expires date, Status badge, Notes, and Delete button
- Status badges display: **Current** (green), **Expires in Xd** (yellow for 60 days or less), or **Expired** (red)

### Step 3: Manage Records

- **Export CSV**: Click the Export CSV button to download all records as a comma-separated values file
- **Delete a record**: Click the × button next to any record to remove it
- **Clear All**: Click the red Clear All button to remove all records (requires confirmation)

### Step 4: Monitor Renewals

- Check the status badges regularly to identify certifications approaching expiry
- The record count at the top shows total staff members tracked
- Records are automatically sorted with soonest expiring certifications listed first

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Where is my data stored?
The data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No information is uploaded to any server, shared with third parties, or accessible outside your current browser on this device.

### Q2: Will I lose my data if I clear my browser cache?
Clearing your browser's localStorage will delete all records. We recommend regularly exporting your data as a CSV file for backup.

### Q3: Can I use this tool on multiple devices?
Data is stored per browser per device. To use across devices, export the CSV from one device and manually re-enter records on another. There is no cloud sync feature.

### Q4: How does the status badge work?
The status badge checks the expiry date against today's date. If expired, it shows "Expired" in red. If expiring within 60 days, it shows "Expires in Xd" in yellow. Otherwise, it shows "Current" in green.

### Q5: Can I edit a record after adding it?
The current version does not support editing records. To update a record, delete it and add a new one with the corrected information.

### Q6: What certification types are available?
The tool includes OSHA BBP (annual), Red Cross BBP (annual), ProTrainings BBP (annual), NHF Bloodborne Pathogens, Custom (annual), and Custom (biennial).

### Q7: How do I change the renewal period for a specific certification?
Select the certification type first. Annual certifications auto-set to 12 months, biennial to 24 months. You can manually adjust the renewal months field for custom periods between 1 and 60 months.

### Q8: Can I export the data?
Yes, click the Export CSV button to download all records as a CSV file. The file includes headers: Name, Certification, Completed, Expires, Renewal Months, and Notes.

### Q9: Is this tool free to use?
Yes, the BBP & Infection Control Training Tracker is completely free. There are no accounts, subscriptions, or hidden fees.

### Q10: How many staff members can I track?
There is no hard limit. The tool stores all records in localStorage, which typically allows several megabytes of data, sufficient for hundreds or thousands of records.
